ENVATURE

Problem - I have seen many people throwing trash on roadsides , in empty land , etc.. which is polluting our land and many people are wasting water , which is one of the major problems in the world  and some farmers don’t know which plant should be grown in their area .

Solution - I will make an app in which there will be some gamification to reduce land pollution and to improve the idea of saving water . After playing the game they should complete a task , and they can upload the photo of completing the task in the community .They will be rewarded with some points after completing the task. I will help the farmers by telling them which crop should be grown in their area by knowing their district , state through some UI . If farmers have any queries they can say their queries through a query screen . Their queries will be stored in my firestore , along with their email id and phone number . I will address them and clarify their doubts
